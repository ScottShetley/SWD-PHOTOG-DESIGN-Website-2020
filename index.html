Recent Projects

DOORMAN

DOORMAN is a business app I designed after watching my friends' businesses open back up after the COVID 19 restrictions were lifted. DOORMAN allows business owners to count customers as they come in through their POS systems. The business owner can set an occupancy limit and when that limit is reached the business is alerted to it. At the same time, DOORMAN sends out a message across all social media accounts to let potential customers know of wait times. (APP IS STILL UNDER CONSTRUCTION)

Simple Machine Brewing Company website

Simple Machine Brewing Company approached me to add on to their website using a template already live on the internet. I stripped it down to its bare bones, added social media interactions using custom JavaScript, HTMl and CSS. They wanted an almost indutrial site that didn't take away from their beer and images of their beer. I think I got the balance correct. The new site went live a weeks before the COVID 19 shutdown. Simple Machine had to pivot their business model and it had to reflect on the site. We added online ordering and delivery services.

Clear Focus Extension

I noticed that I did better work when I had something that helped me refocused several times a day. So I created the Clean Focus Extension and placed it on the Chrome store for public use. The extension allows you to adjust the name and the message that is displayed on the screen. It uses local storage in the browser and local location information to set the time and to determine what photo to show depending on what time f the day it is.

Issue Tracker

The Issue Tracker built with pure Javascript is exactly what it says it is. It is an issue tracker. You can add and delete issues as they arise. The Issue Tracker uses the browsers local storage to keep the issues up to date.
